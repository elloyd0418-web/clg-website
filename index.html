Nano Coating Technology

The Future of
Asset Protection.

Nano coatings are not a gimmick — they are a materials science breakthrough with measurable, documented performance across surface protection, energy efficiency, and environmental impact. CLG holds the only patented electrostatic drone application system, enabling us to coat at scale with precision no traditional method can match.

Patented Technology
Electrostatic Drone Application

Our patented system charges nano coating particles electrostatically, achieving uniform coverage on complex geometries — facades, roofs, towers, hulls — at a fraction of the time and cost of manual application. The only system of its kind.

TiO2 Formulation
Titanium Dioxide Coatings

Photocatalytic TiO2 coatings actively break down organic matter, pollutants, and biological growth when exposed to UV light. Surfaces stay cleaner longer, reducing maintenance frequency and cost while actively improving local air quality.

Zinc Oxide
Anti-Microbial & UV Protection

Zinc oxide nano coatings provide broad-spectrum antimicrobial protection and UV-reflective properties — extending surface life, reducing heat absorption, and providing measurable environmental benefit across large-area applications.

ROI Case
Asset Life & Cost Reduction

Properly applied nano coatings extend asset service life by reducing oxidation, biological fouling, and UV degradation. For large portfolios — commercial real estate, marine fleets, stadiums — the lifecycle cost reduction is material and measurable.
