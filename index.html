The Problem

Large asset owners are making billion-dollar decisions on incomplete data.

HVAC systems consuming 40% of commercial energy while envelope failures go undetected for years. Roof systems carrying ponding water that no one has quantified. Drone vendors collecting data that no one can interpret. Maintenance crews applying coatings that degrade in 18 months.

The cost isn't the vendor bill. The cost is what you didn't know.

  • 01 Energy loss goes undetected. Thermal bridges, failed insulation, and air infiltration points are invisible to ground-based inspection — and cost asset owners millions annually in avoidable HVAC spend.
  • 02 Roof failures are predictable — and preventable. Ponding water adds structural load, accelerates membrane degradation, and precedes catastrophic failure. We identify it years before it becomes a capital event.
  • 03 Drone spend is largely wasted. Most organizations deploy drone operators who collect data no one can use. The technology is sound. The operators and the strategy behind them rarely are.
  • 04 ESG documentation lacks teeth. Boards, insurers, and regulators require defensible data — not estimates. Most sustainability reporting cannot survive scrutiny.
  • 05 Asset surfaces degrade faster than they should. Without proper protective coatings applied correctly at scale, oxidation, biological fouling, and UV degradation compress asset service life and inflate maintenance costs.
